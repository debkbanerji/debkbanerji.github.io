Music

There are two things in this world I don't think I'll ever get tired of: coding, and listening to music. Sometimes, if I can find time in my busy schedule, I write music about computer science. As depressing as that may sound, some of it is actually quite good, though I may be just a bit biased. Not all of them have been recorded yet, but I'm getting there...
